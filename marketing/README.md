## 📊 Key Findings Summary

### Market Opportunity
- Vietnam solar market growing at 11.58% CAGR
- >127 million mobile internet users (90%+ of internet population)
- High transactional intent in solar-related searches
- Local SEO underutilized by national competitors

### EPCVINA Competitive Position
**Strengths:**
- Strong technical foundation (Astro framework)
- Comprehensive LocalBusiness schema
- Geographic advantage in Hà Nội market

**Gaps to Address:**
- Insufficient blog/educational content (critical gap)
- Weak backlink profile
- Limited local citation presence
- No video content strategy
- Missing FAQ & breadcrumb schema

### 6-Month Targets
- Organic traffic: +150%
- Keywords in Top 10: 10+ (from current 2-3)
- Backlinks (DA>30): 50+
- Contact form submissions: 50+/month
- Domain authority: +5-10 points
