## 5. SEO Best Practices for Vietnam

### Google Ranking Factors Priority (Vietnam 2025)
1. **Mobile-First Indexing** (Critical)
   - 90%+ of users on mobile
   - Mobile page speed crucial
   - Touch-friendly interface essential

2. **Page Speed & Core Web Vitals** (Critical)
   - LCP (Largest Contentful Paint): <2.5s
   - FID (First Input Delay): <100ms
   - CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift): <0.1

3. **User Experience (UX)** (High)
   - Mobile responsiveness
   - Easy navigation
   - Clear CTA placement
   - Fast loading

4. **Content Quality & Relevance** (High)
   - Topical authority
   - E-E-A-T signals (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
   - Keyword optimization for natural language

5. **Local SEO Signals** (High for local businesses)
   - Google Business Profile optimization
   - Local citations
   - Customer reviews
   - Local backlinks

6. **Backlink Profile** (High)
   - Quality over quantity (DA>30 preferred)
   - Relevant domain links
   - Natural link building

7. **Schema Markup & Structured Data** (Medium-High)
   - LocalBusiness schema
   - Organization schema
   - Product/Service schema
   - FAQ schema

### Vietnamese Language SEO Specifics
- **Vietnamese Characters:** Proper diacritical mark handling (á, à, ả, etc.)
- **Long-Tail Keywords:** Vietnamese users prefer phrase-based searches
- **Cultural Relevance:** Content must resonate with Vietnamese business practices
- **Machine Translation:** Avoid—local copywriter essential
- **Keyword Density:** 1-2% for natural feel in Vietnamese content
